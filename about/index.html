I am particularly interested in human sensation, perception, and cognition.

My work spans both fundamental research and applied system development, with the goal of translating scientific knowledge into practical technologies.

Mission

My mission is to bridge research, technology development, and real-world impact, transforming scientific knowledge into meaningful experiences for society.

Vision

I aim to be a person who can investigate, build, and deliver.
I strive to work across disciplines and turn ideas into reality.

Philosophy

01

Build trust through reliability

Meaningful outcomes depend on collaboration, and collaboration rests on trust. I treat communication, responsiveness, and punctuality as fundamentals, not extras.

02

Embrace diverse experiences

Technology that truly helps society requires both depth in a core field and exposure to adjacent areas. I seek experiences that span disciplines and phases, from fundamental research to implementation.

03

Build before overthinking

While I investigate prior work and define problems, I start building once a direction is clear. Prototypes reveal insights that planning alone cannot, and I use them to iterate quickly.

04

Act early and take initiative

I developed this mindset during COVID-19, when opportunities did not come automatically. Since then, I have kept a wide radar, noticed opportunities early, and stepped into them proactively.

05

Work beyond borders

Research and development should ultimately improve lives across the world. I value collaboration with people from diverse backgrounds and actively build that foundation through international experiences.

06

Turn ideas into tangible outputs

Even strong ideas have little impact if they remain abstract. I turn research and development into visible outputs such as papers, releases, and public communication.

07

Stay true to my direction

At key turning points, I have chosen paths aligned with what I genuinely want to pursue rather than following trends. This consistency is a core part of my identity.

08

Act with responsibility

I am able to pursue what I care about because of the support and understanding of others. I do not take that for granted, and I respond with responsibility and steady effort.
