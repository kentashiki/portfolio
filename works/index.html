Presentations

Kenta Shiki, Tsukihi Sasao, Yuhi Hasegawa, Masatoshi Watanabe, Keigo Ushiyama, Tomohiro Amemiya. FocuSpeed: Adaptive control of audio playback speed through focus estimation based on biosignals. Human Interface Symposium 2025 Student Contest SICHI2025 September 10, 2025 Encouragement Award
HCI EEG Neurofeedback
We developed a system that estimates a user's focus level in real time from EEG signals and plays audio content at an adaptive speed. The system is intended to reduce missed information caused by declines in concentration while improving the efficiency of audio-based learning.
